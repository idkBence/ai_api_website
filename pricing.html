Frequently Asked Questions

Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
Yes! There are no long-term contracts for our Starter and Pro plans. You can cancel or downgrade your subscription directly from your dashboard at any time.
What happens if I exceed my API limits?
If you're on the Pro plan, we will notify you via email when you reach 80% and 100% of your limits. Overages are billed at $0.001 per extra call, or you can upgrade to Enterprise for unlimited access.
Is my data secure?
Absolutely. We are SOC2 Type II compliant. All data sent to our APIs is encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest. We do not use your data to train our public models unless you explicitly opt-in.
Do you offer volume discounts?
Yes, for high-volume needs, our Enterprise plan offers significant volume discounts. Please contact our sales team to discuss your specific requirements.
